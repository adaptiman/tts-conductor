Welcome. I'm your Instapaper Assistant, and this is a demonstration of my capabilities. Instapaper Assistant is a 100% audio-controlled bot that can navigate, read, annotate, archive, and delete Instapaper articles. I can do all of these tasks simply by listening to your audio commands.

If you are like me, you love Instapaper. It simplifies how I can capture and manage online content. I can highlight excerpts in Instapaper articles using a mouse or touch. But there's a problem. As good as Instapaper is, it still requires interaction via the app or web to use it effectively. While the app has a read feature that uses local text-to-speech generation, the voice is less than optimal - certainly not as nice as mine - and the app still requires manual intervention to use it effectively. It's also "one-way", that is, there are no speech-only controls.

Wouldn't it be nice if you could read, annotate, archive, and delete your Instapaper articles using just your voice? That's where I come in. But before we jump into the features, how about a little bit of background on why my designer wanted a 100% audio interface to Instapaper.
